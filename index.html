Hamsters are belong to the rodent family and there are many different hamster species in the world. Hamsters already become most popular small pet, since they are easy to breeding and care for. Most hamsters inhabit semi-desert areas where they live in burrows that consist of many tunnels. Hamsters are nocturnal, they always sleeping during the daytime, and being most active at night. They have poor eyesight but they have excellent sense of smell and hearing. Most hamsters will use their expandable cheek pouches to carry food and bring back to their burrow, then eat it later, that's their natural instincts.

Hamsters is short lives animal (brief life). The Syrian hamster can lives between two and three years, while dwarf hamster only can lives a year or two. Syrian hamster like to live alone. If syrian hamster live with other hamster in the same cage, they will fight each other until one of them die, so, never keep syrian hamster in group. Dwarf hamster can live with other hamster in the same cage, but not recommend.
