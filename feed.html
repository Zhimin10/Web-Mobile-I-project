Most people will buy the hamster mix food from the pet store to feed their hamster, but not all the people know hamster also like fruit and vegetable.

For the fruit and vegetable:
     ✗ Feed large amount of fruit and vegetables will cause diarrhoea.
     ✗ If hamster shows sign of diarrhoea, you need to stop feed any fruit and vegetables until hamster has recovered.
     ✗ Must wash all the fruit and vegetables before feeding, make sure it is free from pestcides.

Fruit and vegetable that hamster can eat:

Apple(seedless) Banana Corn Carrots
Lychee Kiwi Peas Peach
