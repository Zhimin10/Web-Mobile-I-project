My Daily Care

♥ ♥ ♥  I always place the cage in quiet place, not front of window and not front of heat. Take away from other pets, such as cat and dog, it will reduce the stress of hamster.
♥ ♥ ♥  I change bedding every night, and keep cage clean.
♥ ♥ ♥  Providing enough food and water. Change water every day if possible to keep the water fresh, or once every 3 days maximum.
♥ ♥ ♥  Spend time to play with my hamster, stroke them, feed some fruit or vegetable, try to be close to them.
